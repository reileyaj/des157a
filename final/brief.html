While usability testing, I plan to test with the design tutor, a non-design friend and family member who is unfamiliar with the project. This way I can identify issues that the average user might have, rather than someone that's already familiar with the ins and outs of good and bad form design. Although the visual design is not my main focus, I plan to also run it by the design tutor and a professor.
